Comprehensive Guide to Plant Photosynthesis Process

What is Photosynthesis?

Photosynthesis is a complex biochemical process where plants, algae, and certain bacteria convert light energy into chemical energy. This process occurs in chloroplasts and involves a series of chemical reactions that transform carbon dioxide and water into glucose and oxygen.
